Target Audience Discription:

  • Who: Retired individuals, the working middle class, stay at home moms, and those invested heavily in the community
  • Age: 31 to 69.
  • Technology: Mobile phone, desktop, lop top and tablet users.
  • income: Income levels will be about 30,000 annually to 75,000 annually.
  • Motivation: The desire to stay in touch with the local community and to keep tabs on the ever changing weather.
